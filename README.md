# LinkedIn In a Nutshell

#### **Description**
Ever scroll through LinkedIn and think… “This could be more AI-y”? 
Now it is. *LinkedIn In a Nutshell* replaces all your mundane posts, corporate jargon, 
and profile pics with pure AI buzzwords and shiny AI company logos. 

Welcome to the future of networking. Where we summarized the slum of Corporate into a single, glorious AI-slop experience.

#### **Features**

* Every single piece of text is now a buzzword or the magical word `"BUZZWORD"`.
* Every image, from profile pics to icons, is now an AI logo. Because why not.
* Dynamic content? We got you. Infinite scroll still works, but now in *maximum AI mode*.
* Works in Chrome and Opera because we like to reach the masses.

#### **Installation**

1. Clone or download this masterpiece.
2. Open `chrome://extensions` or `opera://extensions`.
3. Enable **Developer Mode**.
4. Hit **Load unpacked** and select the folder.
5. Refresh LinkedIn and bask in the AI glory.

#### **File Structure**

* `content.js` – The sorcery that turns everything into AI.
* `manifest.json` – Extension config, boring but required.
* `logos/` – Logos of AI overlords ready to replace your images.
* `package.json` – So my IDE doesn't throw a tantrum about types.

#### **Usage**
Open LinkedIn. Watch as your feed, sidebars, and even tiny icons transform into a fully AI-fied experience. 
Every new post you scroll into existence automatically gets the magic treatment.

#### **Warnings**

* Inline SVGs are left alone because some UI elements kinda need to stay functional.
* Your connections might be confused, but that’s the point.
